Tab Ultra

A Chrome extension dedicated to helping you keep tabs in check.

Organize

Easily place groups of tabs into a folder with a few clicks. Organize and modify groups whenever you want in the popout window.

Save Memory

Keep less tabs open at once, using less memory and letting your computer run faster.

Conveniently Open and Close
